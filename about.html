ABOUT US

The Andrew Chronicles is a three-part series about the challenges and inner workings of a case that went through nearly ALL of the levels of the Idaho justice system including a million dollar bail, two trials, a hung jury, a conviction, and an ongoing appeal to the Idaho Supreme court.

All of this for one sarcastic remark during a heated romantic breakup.

Under Idaho law (Idaho Code Â§ 18-109), a crime is defined as "any act committed or omitted in violation of a law forbidding or commanding it, which is punishable upon conviction by penalties such as death, imprisonment, fines, or removal from office." It doesn't matter if you knew or didn't know something was illegal.

"We" are the people behind the book. For now, I am using only first names (for other than the author) to protect personal information.
Andrew is the victim in the story--the victim of an unjust and chaotic legal system that favors the prosecution--not justice. It is a game of who will win and the system has all the cards on their side.
Nancy Watson is the author of this true crime/justice book--but only as a reporter of the events. She is also the Manager/Owner of Guiding Lantern Press.
