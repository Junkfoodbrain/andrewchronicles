THE TRUE STORY OF ANDREW'S PLUNGE INTO THE ABYSS OF THE IDAHO CORRECTIONAL SYSTEM

RELEASE DATE: June 9th, 2026

LANGUAGE: English

FORMAT: Paperback

PUBLISHER: Guiding Lantern Press, LLC

CATEGORY: Biography, True Crime

The story puts YOU in the jury box! Would you convict or acquit?

On a sub-freezing December night in 2023, Andrew discovered the heartbreaking truth: his girlfriend had cheated. After a cycle of betrayals, his dreams were dashed, and he wanted out of the toxic relationship.

He wanted her out of his house. But she wouldn't leave, fighting to retain her hold on his heart even though the lies were too constant and the hurt too deep. In his pain, Andrew made a comment he never intended to act upon-and that put him in prison for life.

The Light in a Dark Corner is more than a true crime memoir. It provides a clear view of the inner workings of a high-profile case where the justice system is weaponized against the defendant. From an attempt to paint Andrew with the spectre of Bryan Kohberger to the misuse of an "Alford Plea" as evidence, this meticulous account reveals how a rash, hyperbolic comment "if you kill him" put Andrew in prison for life.

How could he keep a light of hope burning in the dark abyss of the Idaho correctional SYSTEM?

Now his case sits before the Idaho Supreme Court and the clock is ticking!
