## Report Writing Checklist (Match Rubric Categories)
- Introduction includes: purpose, goals, context, background, and project need.
- Process section includes major steps and evidence artifacts.
- Results section explains outcomes and supporting artifacts.
- Impact section covers project value, future use, and next steps.
- Reflection includes challenges, resolutions, what went well.
- Writing quality: clear flow, correct grammar, direct professional tone.

## Client Recommendation Letter Strategy
- Ask for a short, specific letter that mentions:
	- Reliability and communication.
	- Ability to translate client needs into site features.
	- Technical strengths (organization, maintainability, responsiveness).
	- Overall project value delivered.
- Provide your client a simple prompt to make writing easy:
	- "Please describe the project goals, what I delivered, how I handled feedback, and whether you would recommend me for similar work."
